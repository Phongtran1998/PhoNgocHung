This place is a hidden gem in the dominant Hispanic neighborhood in Northern Virgina. Our restaurant is family-based business; we keep every items in the menu simple yet sophisticated and delicious. Our restaurant is famous for our famous Vietnamese pho noodle soup. Our chef has more than 30 years in experience. Come check out our place and you won't regret!
